# Privacy Policy for FanGlow

**Last Updated: December 20, 2024**

## Introduction

KELDI Inc. ("we", "our", or "us") operates the FanGlow mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our App.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **Banner Content**: Text and messages you create for LED banners are stored locally on your device
- **Preferences**: App settings including language, theme, and display preferences

### Information Collected Automatically
- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system version for compatibility purposes
- **Usage Data**: Anonymous usage statistics to improve app performance

## How We Use Information

We use the collected information to:
- Provide and maintain the App's functionality
- Save your banner presets locally on your device
- Improve user experience and app performance
- Respond to customer support requests

## Data Storage

- **Local Storage**: All banner presets and settings are stored locally on your device using secure storage mechanisms
- **No Cloud Sync**: We do not upload your banner content to external servers
- **No Account Required**: The App does not require user registration or login

## Third-Party Services

The App may use the following third-party services:
- **Expo**: For app development and updates
- **App Store / Google Play**: For app distribution
- **RevenueCat**: For in-app purchase management

## Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your data:
- Local data encryption
- Secure storage APIs provided by iOS and Android

## Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Delete all app data by uninstalling the App
- Clear local storage through the App's settings
- Contact us with questions about your data

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the App.

## Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

- **Email**: cjyh37@gmail.com
- **Developer**: KELDI Inc.
- **Location**: South Korea
